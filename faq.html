Pictures can go on our all cakes. They do not like the cold so we recommend that if you wish to order a fresh cream cake with a picture that you cut the cake on the same day as collection.

It depends on what you order.
Berry Cake: +1 day from purchase if you put it straight into the fridge
ChocolateCake: +2 days from purchase if you keep it in the box and don’t put it in the fridge (unless of course, it’s 30 degrees outside, then you need to treat it like berry cake)
Almond brownies: 3 months if you remove all decoration other than chocolate and wrap well in the fridge
Cookies: 6 months if you remove all decoration and wrap in a sheet of greaseproof paper, tin foil and place in an airtight tin/tub

At Cakes O'clock we do our best to satisfy all tastes. We do have a few vegan cakes and also you can put in a special order for vegan cake if you wish.

The majority of our cakes can be baked overnight. However, there are some cakes for which we need 2 – 3 days’ notice such as our novelty cakes or bespoke cakes. We can find a solution for any cake emergency though if you’ve left it too late. Just give us a call and we’ll do our best.
